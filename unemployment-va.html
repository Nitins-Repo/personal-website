Unemployment Insurance Modernization — Virginia

Role: Solution Architect / SME | Company: HCLA | Period: Aug 2010 – Oct 2012

Served as Solution Architect and Subject Matter Expert for Virginia’s UI modernization initiative, supporting requirements validation, platform alignment, and multi‑team integration efforts as the state transitioned to HCL’s iGovern solution.

Overview

As Solution Architect and SME, I supported the implementation of HCL’s iGovern platform to replace Virginia’s legacy Unemployment Insurance Tax and Benefits system. My work focused on validating requirements, aligning functional and technical teams, and ensuring that the platform met the state’s operational, compliance, and modernization goals.

This engagement required deep UI domain knowledge, cross‑team coordination, and structured analysis to ensure that the new system could support employer services, tax processing, benefits adjudication, and federal reporting requirements.

Key Outcomes

  • Validated and aligned requirements, workflows, and business rules for the iGovern platform
  • Improved coordination between functional, technical, and vendor teams
  • Supported modernization of both Tax and Benefits components of the UI system
  • Strengthened documentation and traceability across program areas

Responsibilities

  • Conducted requirements validation sessions with state SMEs and program leadership
  • Mapped legacy processes to the iGovern platform’s capabilities
  • Supported integration design and cross‑team alignment
  • Provided UI domain expertise across tax, benefits, employer services, and compliance
  • Reviewed functional specifications, workflows, and configuration decisions

Project Scope

  • UI Tax processing and employer contribution workflows
  • Benefits eligibility, adjudication, and payment processes
  • Employer registration and wage reporting
  • Federal reporting and compliance (e.g., wage cross‑match)
  • Platform configuration and integration alignment

Tools & Methods

  • HCL iGovern platform
  • Requirements validation and traceability
  • Process mapping and workflow analysis
  • Cross‑functional team coordination
